Fun and interesting things to me

I enjoy excursions with my family. We have been to places such as Topaz Mtn, Little Sahara sand dunes, Yellowstone, and many other state and national parks. We love the adventure, the campfires, and the memories that are shared.

I am also interested in technology. I have always been fascinated how technology has come to fruition, and also how I could be a role in its development. Having an interest in computers from and early age, and also being very deep-thinking also is leading me to become a patent attorney, so I can help people with not only patents, but also with IP, and copyright.

I like to fix things. I have always liked to restore old, broken or worn items back to their original state. I recently restored a lawn mower that I will be using at my house that I am currently building. I have found that taking in broken items and repairing them gives me a sense of accomplishment, and gives me an appreciation of how they were made, the though behind every part, and how people overcame the possible obstacles and challenges along the path to the items creation.
